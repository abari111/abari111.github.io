SysCov

This project presents a digital people counting system designed with VHDL and tested on an FPGA. It was proposed during the Covid-19 period, in response to the challenges faced by some establishments in enforcing restrictions on the number of people who can be admitted simultaneously. This project was carried out as part of the Programmable Circuits module.

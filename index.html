I am Abari DODO, a junior engineer with expertise in embedded systems and artificial intelligence. I hold an M.Eng in Embedded Systems from the National School of Applied Sciences in Fez, Morocco, and a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1 in France. My work is centered on the convergence of embedded systems and AI, with a strong interest in developing efficient AI models designed to run directly on edge devices.
